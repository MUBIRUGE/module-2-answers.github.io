ABOUT FUFA

FUFA is the body responsible for effective running and control of the National Team and other Sports Clubs in Uganda.This includes all activities in U-16 ,U-18,U-20s including Womens football and other sports

FUFA was founded in the 1970s when football was still a young game in Uganda.

Our main goal is to make Ugandan football reacjh at a certain stage of development and acknoledgement . Our main objective is to export our home grown talent to all countries where football is at a higher standard.
